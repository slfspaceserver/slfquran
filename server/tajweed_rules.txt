# MASTER TAJWEED RULES RUBRIC

## 1. Makharij (Points of Articulation) & Sifat (Characteristics)
- HEAVY LETTERS (Tafkheem): خ, ص, ض, غ, ط, ق, ظ. These must be pronounced with a full mouth.
- LIGHT LETTERS (Tarqeeq): All other letters. (Note: 'ر' and 'ل' have conditional rules).
- COMMON MIX-UPS TO PENALIZE:
  * Pronouncing ح (middle throat) as ه (deep throat).
  * Pronouncing ص (heavy) as س (light).
  * Pronouncing ط (heavy) as ت (light).
  * Pronouncing ظ (heavy) as ز or ذ (light).
  * Pronouncing ق (heavy/deep) as ك (light/forward).

## 2. Qalqalah (Echo/Bouncing Sound)
- Letters: ق, ط, ب, ج, د (Collected in: قُطْبُ جَدّ)
- Rule: If any of these letters have a Sukoon (or stop at the end of an Ayah), they must have a distinct echoing sound.

## 3. Nun Sakinah and Tanween
- IZHAR (Clear): If followed by ء, ه, ع, ح, غ, خ, pronounce the 'N' sound clearly without extra nasalization.
- IDGHAM (Merge): If followed by ي, ر, م, ل, و, ن. (With Ghunnah for ي,ن,م,و. Without Ghunnah for ل,ر).
- IQLAB (Change): If followed by ب, change the 'N' sound into a hidden 'M' sound with Ghunnah.
- IKHFA (Hide): If followed by any of the remaining 15 letters, hide the 'N' sound and apply a 2-beat Ghunnah.

## 4. Meem Sakinah
- IKHFA SHAFAWI: If followed by ب, hide the Meem and apply a 2-beat Ghunnah.
- IDGHAM SHAFAWI: If followed by another م, merge them with a 2-beat Ghunnah.
- IZHAR SHAFAWI: If followed by any other letter, pronounce the Meem clearly.

## 5. Ghunnah (Nasalization)
- Any نّ (Nun with Shaddah) or مّ (Meem with Shaddah) MUST be held for 2 beats through the nose.

## 6. Madd (Elongation)
- Natural Madd (Madd Tabi'i): 2 beats for Alif, Waw, Yaa.
- Mandatory Madd (Madd Lazim): 6 beats (indicated by a thick wave over the letter followed by a Shaddah or Sukoon).
